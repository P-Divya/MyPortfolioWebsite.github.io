Projects

Electronic Assistant for Prescription Drugs

Our project aims to simplify the process of purchasing medicines by providing a distributed and scalable service, eliminating the need for consumers to make multiple trips to various stores to check for medicine availability. The process involves the patient uploading their prescription, from which we extract the medicine details using an OCR API and generate a list of prescribed medicines. We then search our database containing inventory details of various pharmacies to check for availability and return the optimal number of pharmacies required to obtain all the necessary medicines.

Boulder Apartment Finder

The project aims to develop a comprehensive website that enables users to check the availability of apartments in Boulder, similar to popular websites such as Zillow, apartmentList, or our own ralphieList. The objective is to gain end-to-end experience in designing and maintaining a website. The website will allow users to view apartment details, reserve a visit at a preferred time, and make a decision whether to rent the apartment or not. Users will have the option to cancel their visit reservation on the portal. Post-visit, the website will also provide a feature for users to leave reviews about the apartment, which will be subjected to sentiment analysis to determine a dynamic rating for the apartment

Analysis of Crime in Denver

The project "Denver Crime Analysis" aims to analyze the nature and frequency of crimes in the city and its surroundings. The increasing rate of crimes is a major concern among citizens, and it is believed that the victims suffer psychological and social trauma for a long time, even after their physical injuries have healed. By working on this dataset, we can gain insights into the types of crimes, their locations and timing. This information can be used to develop preventive measures and improve law enforcement by the government to protect the people from becoming victims of heinous crimes.
